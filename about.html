Welcome to Pakwan Chennai - the name that coincides with the most exclusive, hygienic and authentic vegetarian experience that one can have. Pakwan is a traditional name that connotes royalty and symbolises dishes prepared by cooks from the greatest kitchens of the Maharajas who loved fine dining and great food.
